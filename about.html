About Us

National Buying Clout

As a Member-Owner of Hardlines Distribution Alliance®, our purchasing agents use over 5 billion dollars in purchasing power to negotiate the best deals on merchandise, insuring that you are competitive in your marketplace.

Monroe Hardware is committed to the success of independent hardware retailers, and can provide you with the tools, programs, and services to help your business grow.

Facilities

Headquartered in Monroe, North Carolina, Monroe Hardware is the largest independent distributor in the Carolinas. Our distribution center combines 200,000 square feet of warehouse space with 150,000 square feet of outside storage, including an extensive pipe yard. Our own fleet of trucks provide weekly on-time deliveries with fees among the lowest in the industry.
